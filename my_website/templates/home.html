William McGee:

Software Developer and Security Professional

While serving as a Security Manager in the U.S. Army, I learned a ton about physical, personnel, and information security. Wanting to fill the gap in my knowledge, I decided to pursue the Cyber Security A.A.S. at Columbus State. During my first two semesters of study, I realized that I had an affinity and passion for programming (and had a few instructors tell me that I should switch), so I changed my major to the Software Development A.A.S.

I enjoyed taking more advanced programming courses and worked as a tutor for introductory computer programming courses during my 3rd semester. I worked part-time as an apprentice at Nationwide during my final semester and graduated in December 2020. After graduating, I was hired on full-time at Nationwide and I now work on the mainframe team.
