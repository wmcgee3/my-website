Studies at the Defense Language Institute - Foreign Language Center

After serving as a medic in the U.S. Army for four years, William volunteered to change his job and become an intelligence analyst. Part of the training for his new job was language school. He was assigned the language of Pashto and was sent to the Defense Language Institute - Foreign language Center at the Presidio of Monterey in California. The Pashto program was 16 months of language, culture, and history training. Upon completion of the program, William was awarded a diploma with honors.
