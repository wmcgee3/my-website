Studies at the Defense Language Institute - Foreign Language Center

After serving as a medic in the U.S. Army for four years, I volunteered to change my job and become an intelligence analyst. Part of the training for my new job was language school. I was assigned the language of Pashto and was sent to the Defense Language Institute - Foreign language Center at the Presidio of Monterey in California. The Pashto program was 16 months of language, culture, and history training. Upon completion of the program, I was awarded a diploma with honors.
